About me

I got genuinely hooked on LLMs around 2023 — not because everyone was talking about them, but because I realized they were actually useful. Like, properly useful. That feeling of getting a model to do something complex that would have taken days to code manually? That never got old.

Most of what I built started from something that bugged me. StyloGuard came from watching AI detectors get easily fooled by paraphrasing tools. The Energy Simulator came from wanting data center carbon costs to feel real, not just numbers in a whitepaper. SkillSynQ came from the frustration of decoding job descriptions by hand.

Now at CMCI, I'm doing the most technically demanding work of my life — healthcare AI where accuracy genuinely matters. That constraint has made me a sharper engineer than any side project ever did.

Still figuring things out. Just faster than before.
